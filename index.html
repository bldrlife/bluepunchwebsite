Features

BluePunch combines punch lists and takeoffs in a single app. No learning curve — open your plan and start working.

  • Punch lists on PDF plans Tap anywhere on your PDF to drop a numbered marker. Add the responsible trade, a description, and a photo. Items are organized by trade for clean handoff.
  • Area, linear, and count takeoffs Calibrate your plan scale once, then tap to measure rooms, runs, and item counts. Results are grouped by label with automatic subtotals. Tuned for standard 24×36 builder sets.
  • Photo capture and markup Take a photo on site and annotate it with arrows, circles, and text. Annotations are burned into the image and included in the exported PDF report.
  • Professional PDF export Export punch lists and takeoffs as branded PDF reports with your company logo, plan overview, photos, and items organized by trade. Share via email, AirDrop, or any app.
  • iOS and Android Native apps on both platforms with iPad support. Your crew can use whatever device they carry.
  • No account. Works offline. Everything stays on your device. No login, no server, no internet required. Open the app in a basement with no signal. It works.
